Security Best Practices

  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Add an extra layer of defense to your vault by requiring a time-based code from an authenticator app.
  • Keep Security Notifications Enabled: By keeping notifications on, you'll receive instant email alerts whenever sensitive actions occur on your account.
  • Mark Secrets as Sensitive: When adding crucial data like bank accounts or master passwords, toggle the "Sensitive" switch. This requires MFA verification before anyone can view or delete the secret.

You can configure all of these settings directly from your Vault Dashboard. Stay secure!
